Scratch Users and the Community


The Scratch Wiki is made by and for Scratchers. Do you want to contribute?


The Scratch community consists of all people who use Scratch: kids, teachers, parents, adults, all united under one programming language and online environment. The community began a very small one in 2007 but has grown to an immense number of users, spanning millions. The community, though united together under Scratch, branches out into various sections.

“ How would you describe the Scratch community in one sentence? ”

– Scratch User

“ A place where kids of all ages create and share projects inspired by their own authentic interests in an informal learning ecosystem. ”

– Lightnin, Scratch Team member
